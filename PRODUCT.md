# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Gift-buyers and slow-living shoppers — people who buy artisan candles for themselves or as meaningful gifts. They seek atmosphere, calm, and a tangible connection to handcraft. They browse in Spanish (with Catalan and English as secondary languages), and they're making a considered, sensory purchase, not a quick utility buy. Context: relaxed, deliberate, often at home.

## Product Purpose

Casa Tierra Luz sells handmade candles with color and scent variants. The product exists to bring warmth, light, and calm into living spaces. Success looks like: a shopper feels the soul of the product before they've even added it to cart — they understand the scent, the material, the intention behind it, and they trust the brand enough to buy.

## Brand Personality

Earthy · Handmade · Calm

The brand is warm and approachable without being loud — it has the restraint of modern minimalism but none of the cold distance. It feels like something made carefully by a real person, not produced by a machine or generated by a trend.

Voice: quiet confidence. Not precious, not performative. Close, direct, honest.

## Anti-references

- Mass-market retail (IKEA, Amazon): sterile catalog grids, zero personality, product-as-SKU
- Cold studio minimalism: all-white, clinical, unapproachable — the "niche perfume brand" distance
- Boho Instagram cliché: terracotta + macramé + pampas grass — the obvious move for "artisan candles"
- AI cream/sand default: the warm-neutral near-white that reads as "generated, not designed"

Reference to move toward: mawastudio.es — refined craft studio aesthetic, elevated without distance. The goal is a brand that feels handmade and close, with the elevated restraint of a serious studio but warmth and approachability that studio portfolios often lack.

## Design Principles

1. **Handcraft, not production** — texture and warmth over clean uniformity. Every detail should suggest a human hand was involved.
2. **Calm through restraint** — space breathes. Minimal elements, generous margins. Don't fill; let things exist.
3. **Sensory storytelling** — candles are scent, light, atmosphere. The interface should evoke those sensations without illustrating them literally.
4. **Approachable warmth** — modern and clean but never cold. The brand welcomes; it doesn't curate at a distance.
5. **Ground in the real** — "Tierra" is earth, material, the physical world. Colors, textures, and copy should feel grounded in something tangible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Sensible defaults. WCAG 2.1 AA as the working baseline: 4.5:1 body text contrast, keyboard navigation, semantic HTML. Reduced-motion alternatives for all animations. No specific formal target level beyond good judgment.
